Many of the planets in our solar system have moons orbiting around them, ranging from small, irregular bodies to large, geologically active worlds. These moons offer valuable insights into planetary formation and evolution.

:::tip[Did you know?]

Jupiter's moon Europa is believed to have a subsurface ocean beneath its icy crust, raising the possibility of extraterrestrial life.

:::
