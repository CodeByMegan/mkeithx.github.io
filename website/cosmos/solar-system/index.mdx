# Solar System
A big star and everything that goes around it because of gravity. Let's dive into the wonders of our solar system, exploring all the amazing things that orbit around our [Sun](./solar-system/sun).





<DocCardList />

- ✅ **[NASA's Eyes](https://eyes.nasa.gov/apps/solar-system/#/home)** : A simulated view our Solar System, the universe beyond and the spacecraft exploring them.
- ✅ **[Solar System Trek](https://trek.nasa.gov/)** :  Access surface elevation profiles, sun angles, 3D print files, and VR experiences in our solar system
- ✅ **[Leolabs](https://platform.leolabs.space/visualizations/leo)** : Satellites, debris, and other objects in low Earth Orbit.
- ✅ **[Planet Hunters](https://planethunters.org)** : Discover new planets around stars outside of our own Solar System.
